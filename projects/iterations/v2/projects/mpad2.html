MinePAD 2 : an eulogy

MinePAD 2 used to be the successor to MinePAD. It was shiny, faster, using a standard format (JSON) to store data, its code was cleaner. MinePAD 2 was everything MinePAD wanted to be. MinePAD 2 could pull data from the Minecraft Wiki, parse it, and convert it so that it could present it. I'd like to take a moment to express how much I hate this wiki where no two pages follow the same conventions and use the same templates.

Yet, on this tragic day of May 2012, it was no more.
I had managed to delete the entire project folder after pushing it to git, deleting it for no reason then deleting the repo. Don't ask why, I don't even know. Here's to MinePAD 2, which I might rewrite one day. MinePAD 2, you will not be forgotten.
